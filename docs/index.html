ZimX A calm place to return to — and a powerful place to organize your thinking. ZimX is a local-first workspace for notes, tasks, and ideas. It can be a quiet journal you visit between meetings. Or it can become your main brain — the one place you trust to hold everything together. You decide the depth. ZimX meets you there. --- A journal when you want it ZimX includes a built-in daily journal — always there, never required. Each day is a simple note page: • A place to jot down plans written earlier • A place to capture what actually happened • A place to land when your day is fragmented across tools You don’t have to “journal.” The page is just there when you need it. --- A safe haven from noisy tools Modern work lives everywhere: Email. Chat. Calendars. Tickets. Docs. Dashboards. ZimX doesn’t try to replace them. It gives you a **home base**. • Capture notes from meetings • Write down decisions once • Track follow-ups where they make sense • Link out to Slack, Teams, Jira, Confluence, docs, and more When you need to remember, you don’t hunt — you return. --- Notes that stay local and last Everything in ZimX lives on your computer as plain text. • No accounts • No subscriptions • No cloud lock-in • Full offline use Your notes remain readable and usable — even outside ZimX. This is software that respects your work. --- Tasks that live inside your notes Tasks in ZimX aren’t a separate system. They’re part of your writing: • A checkbox in a meeting note • A follow-up on today’s page • A reminder linked to a project ZimX can surface what’s due or upcoming — without pulling tasks away from the context that gives them meaning. --- A calendar that reflects real work In ZimX, the calendar is a set of dated note pages — not a grid of time slots. Each day: • May contain plans written earlier • Becomes a running log of what actually happened • Links out to clients, projects, and ideas The calendar gives you the date. ZimX captures the day. --- See your thinking take shape ZimX includes a visual link graph that shows how everything connects: • Days to projects • Notes to ideas • Work to outcomes The graph grows naturally as you write — a living map of how your thinking evolves. It’s there when it helps, invisible when it doesn’t. --- Simple enough to visit. Strong enough to rely on. ZimX works whether you: • Drop in occasionally • Keep it open all day • Use the journal lightly • Let it become your trusted backbone There’s no system to maintain. No pressure to optimize. Just a dependable place to think.
