Journal

You work in time. Your journal is the home base.

Each day is where work begins and where it gets remembered. Capture what you are working on, link into the rest of your graph, and keep a clear record of what was done and why it mattered.

Day pages

Plans, logs, and reflections live together.

Start with a date

A day page begins with what you expected to happen. It can hold tasks, reminders, or a simple outline.

Capture what actually happened

As your day unfolds, notes and decisions are added directly to the page. The day becomes a true log, not a schedule.

Connect it forward

Link meetings, projects, and people as you write. Those links turn the calendar into the backbone of your graph.

Calendar as memory.

The calendar gives you the date. ZimX captures the day. Over time, the calendar becomes a durable record of what mattered and why.

It is not a grid of time slots. It is a living archive of your work and life.
